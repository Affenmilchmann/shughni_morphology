xāy uz-um tarid-ām māš ca yat yakumaθ misol-and māš mardumen wi xuɣ̌nůni as wi taraf ca
xuɣ̌nůni awɣůniston-and dusikaθ farq yāni māš-ta dev kalamot-i rūsi istifodā nakinām
az mi taraf-ta dev istifodā kinen ala kalamoten māš istifodā ca kinām
mumkin wāδ forsi-ti viyen tari istifodā nakinen bādi yakumaθ-ām
ca yat yo qīn-ām sat rostiyaθ yo misol-and
tar dargo-yām bozi čīd ca nax̌toyd
ičidům ӡulik qati ičīz gāp-ām ca lůd yu-yi wi nafāmt
šint-i yo di-ǰāt yu-yi wi nax̌uδǰ yo yakumin bor-i wi x̌uδǰ dis-ga xuɣ̌nůni yo ik-ů māš
ik-wi tarz-ti-yām māš gāp ca δod wi tarz-i
farq vud bād rostiyaθ ӡuliken šīnt-en-at yo
ar čīyaθ-en lůd-i di aro dis-ga nist-at tarůd taram-at
bād amo ostāostā wev dusik farqiyat-en ca
māš mardum-and yo māš ziv-and az wi taraf-at az mi taraf lap ӡulik-en wāδ
amo bād odam ostāostā
fāmt čīz māš lůvām čīz nalůvām x̌o
bād wi dar nazar odam ca zêzd di-ga
ziv-and sof ačaθ čīz nist yo
muškili nist mi dari mardum qati
